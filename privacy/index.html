Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

dyeary is a voice journaling app. You speak, and the app turns what you said into a written entry and a sense of how you were feeling, building a private map of your moods over time. This page explains exactly what we collect, why, who helps us process it, and how you stay in control of it.

Who we are

dyeary is operated by an independent developer (Affan Khokhar). If you have any question about this policy or your data, contact us at affankhokhar@gmail.com.

How your entries are protected

Everything you write and record is encrypted on our servers, twice over. Your data is encrypted at rest by our infrastructure providers, and on top of that, your entry text and audio are encrypted again with a key that is never stored alongside your data. In practice this means there is no screen on our side that shows anyone your entries, including the person who built dyeary. Reading an entry from our systems would require a deliberate, manual act with a separately held key, and our policy is that this happens only with your explicit consent in a support case.

Your words are never used to train AI models. When dyeary processes an entry (transcribing it, titling it, finding the feelings in it), the text passes through our AI processors only for that moment and is not stored by them or used for training.

How we use your data

  • To transcribe your spoken entries and refine them into readable journal entries.
  • To estimate the emotional tone of an entry and build your private mood map.
  • To organize what you talk about into your private Reflections (people, places, and parts of life that recur in your entries).
  • To authenticate you and keep your journal accessible only to you.
  • To operate, secure, debug, and improve the app.

We do not sell your personal data, we do not use your journal content to show you advertising, and we use no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Voice data, specifically

Your audio is captured only while you are actively recording an entry. It is processed to produce your transcript and mood reading, then stored encrypted so you can play your entries back inside the app. Recordings are kept only for playback: deleting an entry deletes its recording, and deleting your account deletes them all.

Data retention and deletion

  • Your entries, recordings, and mood data are kept until you delete them or delete your account.
  • You can delete any individual entry in the app at any time; its recording and mood data are removed with it.
  • You can delete your entire account from inside the app (Profile → Delete account). This permanently removes your entries, recordings, mood data, and your account itself, and frees your email to sign up again.
  • Delete means delete: we do not keep copies of deleted journal content.

Subscriptions

dyeary Plus is an optional subscription billed through your Apple account. Apple handles all payment information; we receive only a signed confirmation that a subscription is active. Manage or cancel it any time in your device’s Settings → Subscriptions.

Children

dyeary is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Deletion is built into the app; for anything else, email affankhokhar@gmail.com.
